About WishGrid

The journey from wishing to having, visualized.

Our Story

WishGrid was born out of a simple observation: in the age of endless online shopping options, keeping track of what we want becomes overwhelming. How many times have you found the perfect item, only to forget about it weeks later?

We created WishGrid in 2025 as a visual solution to this problem. Our platform helps you organize everything you want in one beautiful, customizable grid - making your wishes more tangible and organized.

Whether you're saving for big purchases, planning gifts, or just keeping track of your personal wishlist, WishGrid turns abstract desires into a visual journey you can track, prioritize, and achieve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is WishGrid free to use?

Yes! WishGrid is completely free for all users. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Can I share my wishlist with others?

Currently, WishGrid is designed for personal use. However, we're working on sharing features that will be available in upcoming updates.

How do I add an item to my wishlist?

Simply navigate to your Dashboard and click the "Add New Wish" button. Fill out the form with your item details and save!

Is my data secure?

We take data security seriously. Your wishlist information is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your consent.
